Blackjack Help

Place a bet and click deal!

After the first round, you can bounce your bet back and forth using the "Half" and "Double" controls. These bet controls will also deal simultaneously.

General Strategy

  • Decline Insurance unless you're counting cards
  • Double on 11 with dealer showing less than 7
  • Double on 10 when no face cards/tens are visible and more than 3 players playing
  • Always Split aces, sevens, eights
  • Split nines with dealer showing 9 or less than 8
  • Never Split fives, tens, or face cards
  • Hit soft 13-16, it's a free card.
  • Have 13 or more with dealer showing 3-6; Stand! Let the dealer bust.
  • Otherwise hit till ya get 17, more than 17, or bust; except 13-16 with dealer showing 3-6
